Transport

In the town of Sangjang turn north off the main road Jl. Paokmotong Kotaraja to reach Tetebatu on good roads.

Tetebatu is only 45km from the island's international airport (LOP), with flight links across Indonesia and to international hubs including Singapore and Perth.

With the right transport, you can reach almost anywhere on the island in a day. Popular options include Sembalun, Kuta Lombok, Senggigi or the nearby Gili Islands.
